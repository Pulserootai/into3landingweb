Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to into3.ai ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform and services.

By accessing or using into3.ai, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, grade level, school name
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, learning preferences, subject interests
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication Data: Messages sent to teachers, support tickets, feedback, and survey responses

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, learning progress, quiz scores
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: Cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance user experience
  • AI Interaction Data: Questions asked to AI tutor, topics explored, learning patterns

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Login: If you sign in via Google or other providers, we receive basic profile information
  • School Integration: Information shared by schools or educational institutions (with consent)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: Deliver personalized learning experiences, AI tutoring, and educational content
  • Personalization: Adapt content difficulty, recommend subjects, and customize learning paths
  • Communication: Send important updates, notifications, learning reminders, and promotional content (with consent)
  • Analytics: Analyze usage patterns to improve platform performance and user experience
  • Safety & Security: Detect fraud, prevent abuse, and ensure platform security
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
  • Research: Conduct educational research to improve learning outcomes (anonymized data only)

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We share information when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

4.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe)
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics)
  • Email service providers
  • AI/ML service providers

4.3 Educational Institutions

If you access into3.ai through a school or institution, we may share progress reports, attendance data, and performance metrics with your school (with appropriate consent).

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5. Children's Privacy

Special Protections for Minors: into3.ai is designed for students of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

  • We obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting information from children under 13
  • Parents can review, edit, or delete their child's information at any time
  • We limit the collection of personal information from minors to what is necessary for educational purposes
  • We do not enable public profiles or social features for users under 13 without parental consent

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted over the internet is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure servers with backup and disaster recovery measures

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update your account information at any time through your profile settings.

7.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@into3.ai. Please note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7.3 Opt-Out of Marketing

You can unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email.

7.4 Cookie Management

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Active account data is retained while your account is active
  • Learning progress data is retained to maintain educational continuity
  • Deleted account data is permanently removed within 90 days (except where required by law)
  • Anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for research and analytics

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.
