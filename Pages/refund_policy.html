Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Our Commitment

At into3.ai, we want you to be completely satisfied with your learning experience. This Refund Policy explains when and how you can request a refund for your subscription.

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a full refund if you're not satisfied within the first 7 days of your initial purchase.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

2.1 Eligible for Full Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • First 7 Days: You request a refund within 7 days of your initial subscription purchase
  • Technical Issues: Platform unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours
  • Duplicate Charges: You were charged multiple times for the same subscription
  • Unauthorized Charges: Your payment method was charged without authorization

2.2 Not Eligible for Refund

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following situations:

  • Refund requests made more than 7 days after initial purchase
  • Automatic subscription renewals (unless canceled before renewal date)
  • Simple change of mind after 7-day period has expired
  • Unsatisfactory exam results or learning outcomes
  • Account termination for violating Terms of Service

3. How to Request a Refund

Contact our support team via:

  • Email: refunds@into3.ai
  • Support Portal: Settings → Billing → Request Refund

Include: email address, subscription type, purchase date, reason for refund.

4. Processing Time

  • Review: 2-3 business days
  • Processing: 5-7 business days
  • Bank reflection: Additional 5-10 business days

5. Contact Information

  • Email: refunds@into3.ai
  • Support: support@into3.ai

We're Here to Help: If you're unhappy with into3.ai, please reach out. We'll do our best to resolve your concerns before processing a refund.
