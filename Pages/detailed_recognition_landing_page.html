Got Questions? We Have Answers

Everything you need to know about our recognition programs in one place.

Can I apply for multiple programs simultaneously? +
Yes! If you meet the eligibility criteria for multiple programs, you're welcome to submit separate applications for each. For example, a teacher can apply individually while their school applies for institutional certification. Each application is evaluated independently.
Is there an application fee? +
No, absolutely not! All into3.ai recognition programs are completely free to apply to. We believe financial constraints should never prevent deserving candidates from seeking recognition. There are no hidden charges at any stage of the process.
What happens if I'm not selected this year? +
Don't be discouraged! You can reapply in subsequent years. We provide detailed feedback to all candidates who reach the interview stage, helping you understand areas for improvement. Many of our winners were previous applicants who strengthened their profiles and succeeded in later cycles.
How are winners selected? Is it truly merit-based? +
Absolutely! Our selection process combines AI-powered preliminary screening (eliminating bias) with evaluation by an independent jury of education experts, former award winners, and industry leaders. All jury members sign conflict-of-interest declarations, and scoring is done on a standardized rubric focusing on measurable achievements, impact, and innovation.
When and how are prizes distributed? +
Cash prizes and scholarships are distributed within 30 days of the awards ceremony via direct bank transfer. Physical certificates, trophies, and certification plaques are presented at the ceremony and can also be shipped to winners who cannot attend. Institutional benefits like AI tools access are activated immediately after the ceremony.
Do I need to attend the awards ceremony? +
While attendance is not mandatory, we highly encourage it! The ceremony is a fantastic networking opportunity where you'll meet education leaders, policy makers, and fellow winners. Travel support (up to ‚Çπ15,000) is provided to winners coming from outside the host city. If you absolutely cannot attend, arrangements can be made for virtual participation.
Can teachers from private coaching centers apply? +
Yes! We recognize excellence across all forms of education. Teachers from government schools, private schools, coaching centers, online platforms, and even independent educators are eligible. What matters is your teaching impact, innovation, and the success stories of your students.
Are there regional quotas or reservations? +
We follow a purely merit-based selection process with no regional quotas. However, we do ensure representation from diverse backgrounds, geographies, and institutional types in our shortlists through balanced screening. Special consideration is given to applicants from underserved areas who have created exceptional impact despite resource constraints.
