Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in to your account
  • Security: Protect against fraud and ensure platform security
  • Session Management: Maintain your session across pages

These cookies cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience:

  • Preferences: Remember your language, theme, and settings
  • Features: Enable interactive features and personalized content
  • Video Player: Remember playback settings and progress

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use our platform:

  • Usage Data: Track pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • Performance: Monitor platform performance and identify issues
  • Improvements: Help us improve user experience

We use Google Analytics and similar tools for this purpose.

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used for advertising:

  • Targeting: Show relevant ads based on your interests
  • Retargeting: Display ads on other websites you visit
  • Measurement: Track effectiveness of marketing campaigns

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from third parties that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Payment Processors: Razorpay, Stripe for secure payments
  • Social Media: Share buttons and social features
  • Video Hosting: YouTube or Vimeo embedded videos

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

4.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

4.2 Our Cookie Consent Tool

When you first visit into3.ai, you'll see a cookie banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences

Change your preferences anytime through Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences.

4.3 Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics: Use Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Advertising: Visit Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • You may need to log in more frequently
  • Preferences won't be saved
  • Some features may not work properly
  • Personalized content may not be available

6. Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain for a set period (usually 1-12 months)

7. Contact Us

For questions about our use of cookies:

  • Email: privacy@into3.ai
  • Support: support@into3.ai

Your Choice Matters: You have full control over cookie preferences. We respect your privacy and will never share your data without consent.
