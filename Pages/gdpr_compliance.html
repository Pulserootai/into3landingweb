GDPR Compliance

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Our Commitment to GDPR

into3.ai is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This page explains how we comply with GDPR requirements and your rights under this regulation.

GDPR Applies If: You are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), including the EU, Iceland, Liechtenstein, and Norway.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

2.1 Consent

We obtain your explicit consent for marketing communications, cookies (non-essential), and sharing data with third parties for purposes beyond service delivery.

2.2 Contract Performance

Processing necessary to fulfill our contract with you (providing educational services, account management, billing).

2.3 Legal Obligation

Processing required to comply with legal obligations (tax reporting, responding to lawful requests).

2.4 Legitimate Interests

Processing necessary for our legitimate interests (improving services, fraud prevention, analytics) where your rights do not override these interests.

3. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

3.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will provide this information in a structured, machine-readable format within 30 days.

3.2 Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. You can update most information through your account settings.

3.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data when:

  • Data is no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected
  • You withdraw consent and there's no other legal basis for processing
  • You object to processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds
  • Data was processed unlawfully

3.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances:

  • You contest the accuracy of the data
  • Processing is unlawful but you don't want erasure
  • We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims
  • You've objected to processing pending verification

3.5 Right to Data Portability

You can request your personal data in a portable format and have it transmitted to another service provider where technically feasible.

3.6 Right to Object

You can object to:

  • Processing based on legitimate interests
  • Direct marketing (including profiling)
  • Processing for scientific or historical research

3.7 Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making

You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, that produce legal or similarly significant effects. Our AI tutor provides recommendations but final educational decisions remain with you.

4. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any GDPR rights:

  • Email: gdpr@into3.ai or privacy@into3.ai
  • Account Settings: Many rights can be exercised through your account dashboard
  • Written Request: Send to our Data Protection Officer (details below)

We will respond within 30 days. If your request is complex, we may extend this by an additional 60 days with notification.

5. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

We have appointed a Data Protection Officer to oversee GDPR compliance:

  • Email: dpo@into3.ai
  • Mail: Data Protection Officer, into3.ai, [Company Address]

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred outside the EEA for processing. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): EU-approved model contracts
  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate data protection
  • Binding Corporate Rules: For transfers within our corporate group

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Duration of account + 90 days
  • Legal Requirements: As required by law (e.g., 7 years for tax records)
  • Anonymized Data: Retained indefinitely for research

8. Children's Data

For users under 16, we obtain parental consent before processing personal data. Parents can access, rectify, or delete their child's data at any time.

9. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will:

  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours
  • Notify affected individuals without undue delay
  • Provide information about the breach and remedial actions

11. Complaints and Supervisory Authority

If you believe we've violated GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority:

  • Find your local authority at: edpb.europa.eu
  • We encourage you to contact us first so we can address your concerns

12. Updates to This Policy

We will notify EEA users of material changes to our GDPR compliance practices via email and prominent website notice.

13. Contact Information

For GDPR-related inquiries:

  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@into3.ai
  • GDPR Requests: gdpr@into3.ai
  • General Privacy: privacy@into3.ai

Your Privacy Rights Matter: We take GDPR compliance seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. If you have any concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out.
